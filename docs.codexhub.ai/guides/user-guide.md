## Getting Started

### System Requirements

The Digital Banking Platform is a web application that works in all modern browsers:
- Google Chrome (recommended)
- Mozilla Firefox
- Safari
- Microsoft Edge

### Accessing the Platform

1. Open your web browser and navigate to the platform URL
2. Click on "Sign In" if you have an account or "Sign Up" to create a new one

### Creating an Account

1. Click "Sign Up" from the main page
2. Fill in your details:
   - Name
   - Email address
   - Phone number
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
3. Submit the form to create your account
4. You will be automatically logged in and redirected to the dashboard

### Logging In

1. Navigate to the "Sign In" page
2. Enter your registered phone number and password
3. Click "Sign In" to access your account

## Account Management

### Profile Settings

You can view and manage your profile information from the dashboard by clicking on your profile icon in the top-right corner.

### Security Settings

We recommend regularly updating your password and enabling any additional security features that may be available.

## Dashboard

The dashboard is your main interface for the Digital Banking Platform. It displays:

- Your current balance
- Recent transaction history
- Balance chart showing your financial activity over time
- Quick action buttons for common tasks

### Balance Information

Your balance is prominently displayed at the top of the dashboard. This shows your available funds that can be used for transfers and transactions.

### Balance Chart

The balance chart visualizes your account balance changes over time, helping you track your financial activity.

## Transactions

The transactions page shows your complete transaction history, including:

- Money added to your account (on-ramp transactions)
- Peer-to-peer transfers (sent and received)
- Transaction status, amount, date, and details

### Transaction Types

- **Processing**: Transaction is being processed
- **Success**: Transaction completed successfully
- **Failure**: Transaction failed to complete

## Peer-to-Peer Transfers

The platform allows you to send money directly to other users within the system.

### Sending Money

1. Navigate to the P2P page from the sidebar
2. Enter the recipient's phone number
3. Enter the amount you wish to send
4. Review the transaction details
5. Confirm the transfer

### Transfer Limitations

- You cannot send more money than your available balance
- You cannot send money to yourself
- The recipient must be a registered user of the platform

## Adding Money

You can add funds to your account through the on-ramp feature.

### Adding Funds

1. Navigate to the dashboard
2. Click on "Add Money" card
3. Select your bank or payment provider
4. Enter the amount you wish to add
5. Follow the provider-specific instructions to complete the transaction

### Supported Payment Providers

The platform supports multiple payment providers for adding money to your account. The specific providers available may vary based on your region.

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**I can't log in**
- Verify you're using the correct phone number
- Reset your password if you've forgotten it

**My transfer failed**
- Check your available balance
- Verify the recipient's phone number
- Try again later if the system is experiencing technical issues

**My added funds aren't showing up**
- On-ramp transactions may take some time to process
- Check the transaction status in your transaction history
- Contact support if a "Success" transaction isn't reflected in your balance

### Getting Help

If you encounter any issues not covered in this guide, please contact our support team at support@digitalbanking.example.com.
